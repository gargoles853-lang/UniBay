About UniBay

Your comprehensive guide to Philippine Higher Education Institutions

Our Mission

UniBay is dedicated to providing students, parents, and educators with easy access to accurate and up-to-date information about universities and colleges across the Philippines. We aim to simplify the search for higher education opportunities by consolidating data from official sources into one user-friendly platform.

Key Features

200+ HEIs

Comprehensive database of universities and colleges from all regions of the Philippines.

Course Offerings

Detailed academic programs for major institutions, from undergraduate to graduate levels.

Interactive Maps

Visualize university locations across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

AI Assistant

Get instant answers to your questions about universities, courses, and admissions.

Favorites

Save and organize your preferred institutions for easy access.

Dark Mode

Comfortable viewing day or night with theme switching.

Data Sources

  • CHED Commission on Higher Education – official listings and program data via ched.gov.ph
  • University Websites Official course offerings and contact information from individual HEI websites
  • Geolocation Coordinates sourced from OpenStreetMap and university directories
  • CHED Press Releases Recent updates and policy announcements (March 2026)

Technology Stack

  • Frontend HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Maps Leaflet.js with CARTO Voyager tiles
  • AI Google Gemini API (gemini-pro) with local data augmentation
  • Icons Font Awesome 6, Google Fonts (Inter, Playfair Display)
